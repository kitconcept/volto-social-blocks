## ðŸš€ Getting Started

Each block is easy to use:

1. Add the block to your Plone page
2. Paste the URL from the social media platform
3. Configure options like size, alignment, and appearance
4. The content will be automatically embedded

## ðŸŽ¨ Common Features

Most blocks support:

- **Responsive sizing** - Small, medium, and large sizes
- **Flexible alignment** - Left, center, or right alignment
- **Theme options** - Light and dark modes (where applicable)
- **Native players** - Full playback and interaction controls
